POSITION SUMMARY:
We are looking for a talented Data Scientist to join our analytics team. The successful candidate will leverage machine learning, statistical modeling, and data visualization to drive business insights and support strategic decision-making across the organization.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Develop and deploy machine learning models for predictive analytics and recommendation systems
- Conduct exploratory data analysis to identify trends and patterns in large datasets
- Build data pipelines and ETL processes using Python and SQL
- Create interactive dashboards and visualizations using Tableau and Power BI
- Collaborate with business stakeholders to define analytical requirements
- Perform A/B testing and statistical analysis to evaluate product features
- Document methodologies and findings in technical reports
- Present insights and recommendations to senior leadership
- Mentor junior data analysts and interns
- Stay updated on latest ML/AI techniques and tools

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:
- Master's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related field
- 3+ years of experience in data science or analytics role
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL
- Experience with machine learning libraries (scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Solid understanding of statistical methods and experimental design
- Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or matplotlib)
- Knowledge of big data technologies (Spark, Hadoop, or Hive)
- Experience working with relational and NoSQL databases
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:
